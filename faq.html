Frequently Asked Questions

How Do I Order?

You can order by phone, email or through out online store, coming soon!

How Long Does Shipping Take?

Handling takes no more than one business day and all orders are shipped overnight so you can expect your order within two business days of ordering!

What if I Order The Wrong Product?

If you find that you ordered the wrong product, just send us an email explaing the situation and we will gladly refund the purchase and you may keep the product.

Why Does Pastry Flour Take Longer to Process?

Pastry flour takes an extra day to process because we have to run it throught the pixie dust machine to ensure that it has all the magical ingredients for making delicious pastries and delacacies!
